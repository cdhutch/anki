# B737 Type Rating — Anki Flashcard Deck

A community flashcard deck for Anki covering B737 aircraft systems, procedures, and limitations. Intended as a study supplement for type rating candidates and recurrent training.

> **YMMV.** These cards reflect one person's interpretation of publicly available B737 technical material. Every fleet, every sim, every check airman is different. Live by the gouge, die by the gouge.

---

## Disclaimer

These notes are not based on any particular operator's documentation, training program, or standard operating procedures. They are derived from publicly available B737 technical references and are provided for **general study purposes only**.

**Always verify against your operator's current approved references** — your airline's AOM/FCOM revision, SOPs, training standards, and check ride expectations are the authoritative source. This deck does not constitute type rating instruction, is not approved by any aviation authority, and may contain errors, omissions, or information that conflicts with your operator's standards.

Use at your own risk.

---

## What's Included

- **Systems Verification Exam (SVE)** — multiple-choice questions covering pneumatics, engines, autoflight, electrical, hydraulics, and more
- **Limits** — key operational and structural limits
- **Triggers & Flows** — flows and memory items
- **Cats & Dogs** — miscellaneous procedures, configurations, and "good to know" items
- **Mnemonics** — memory aids for checklists and flows

## How to Import

### Desktop

1. Download the `.apkg` file from the [Releases](../../releases) page
2. Open Anki
3. Go to **File → Import**
4. Select the downloaded `.apkg` file
5. Click **Import**

The deck will appear in your deck list under **B737**.

### Mobile (AnkiMobile / AnkiDroid)

**Option A — AnkiWeb sync (recommended):**
1. Import on desktop first (above)
2. Sync your desktop Anki to AnkiWeb (**Sync** button)
3. On mobile, open the app and sync — the deck will appear automatically

**Option B — Direct import:**
1. Download the `.apkg` file to your device
2. Open the file with AnkiMobile or AnkiDroid (tap **Open with** → Anki)
