# front_md

**Runaway Stabilizer**

Recall items?

# back_md

<ol>
  <li>Control column ... Hold firmly</li>
  <li>Autopilot (if engaged) ... Disengage</li>
  <li>Autothrottle (if engaged) ... Disengage</li>
  <li>Control column and thrust levers ... Control aircraft pitch attitude and airspeed</li>
  <li>Main Electric Stabilizer trim ... Reduce control column forces</li>
  <li><strong>If</strong> the runaway <strong>stops</strong> after the autopilot is disengaged: Do <strong>not</strong> re-engage the autopilot or autothrottle</li>
</ol>

<div class="qrc-separator">■ ■ ■ ■</div>

<ol start="7">
  <li>
    <strong>If</strong> the runaway <strong>continues</strong> after the autopilot is disengaged:
    <ul>
      <li>STAB TRIM cutout switches (both) ... CUTOUT</li>
      <li>
        <strong>If</strong> the runaway <strong>continues</strong>:
        <ul>
          <li>Stabilizer trim wheel ... Grasp and hold</li>
        </ul>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ol>
