# back_md

**Discontinued Approach — DAVS**

- **D:** Detune — deselect ILS frequency (NAV 1 and NAV 2)
- **A:** Altitude — check/set
- **V:** Vertical Speed — select/set V/S
- **S:** Steer — heading check/set, then select HDG or LNAV
