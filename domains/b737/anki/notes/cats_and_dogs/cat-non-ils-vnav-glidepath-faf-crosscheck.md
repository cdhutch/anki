# front_md

During a non-ILS approach, what must the pilot cross-check the VNAV glidepath against? What is the tolerance? What happens if it isn't met?

# back_md

Cross-check **VNAV glidepath** against **FAF/FAP crossing altitude**

Tolerance: **±100 ft**

If not met: downgrade to **LNAV minimums** and comply with **stepdown altitude constraints**
