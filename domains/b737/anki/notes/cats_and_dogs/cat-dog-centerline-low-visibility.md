# front_md

When is the "Centerline" call used in low visibility takeoffs and landings, and what is its implication?

# back_md

**When:** Called when the aircraft is no longer tracking the localizer.

**Implication:** Possible runway incursion distorting the localizer signal.

The callout serves as an early warning of potential signal corruption from obstacles on or near the runway, prompting immediate crew action to verify approach stability and runway status.
