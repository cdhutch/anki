# front_md

What approach **category** is the B737, and what is the exception?

# back_md

**Category D**

Exception — may use **Category C** minimums if **all** of:
- Short Field Package installed
- Straight-in approach
- Flaps 40
- Approach speed ≤ 140 KIAS
