# front_md

Following an ENGINE FIRE, Engine Severe Damage or Separation, or Engine Limit / Surge / Stall — when is the non-normal checklist accomplished?

# back_md

When:

- Aircraft and flight path are under control
- Gear has been retracted
- Safe altitude attained (minimum 400 feet AGL)
