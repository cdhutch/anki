**74CBT** — when are autobrakes required on landing?

# back_md

**74CBT**

- **7** — Runway length &lt; 7,500'
- **4** — RVR &lt; 4,000'
- **C** — Contaminated or wet surface
- **B** — Braking action less than Good
- **T** — Tailwind
