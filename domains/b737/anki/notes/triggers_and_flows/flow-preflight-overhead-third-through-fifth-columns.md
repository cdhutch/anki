# Back

**Preflight — Overhead Panel (3rd–5th Columns)**

3rd column:
- All switches: AFT or GUARDED
- Wipers: PARKED

4th column:
- Window heat: ON × 4, green
- Probe heaters: AUTO; amber lights × 8
- Anti-ice switches: OFF × 3, no lights
- Engine-driven hydraulic pumps: ON × 2
- CVR: PUSH TO TEST, green light

5th column:

Temperature control panel:
- TRIM AIR: ON (forward)
- Air temperature knobs: 12:00
- Blue lights: ON × 2; no amber lights unless APU BLEED ON

Bleed / pack panel:
- L/R recirc fans: AUTO
- Isolation valve: OPEN
- Bleed air switches: ON

If using PCA:
- Pack switches L/R: OFF

If using APU bleed:
- Pack switches L/R: AUTO or HIGH
- APU BLEED: ON
- No amber lights except DUAL BLEED

Cabin pressurization:
- FLT ALT and LAND ALT: ENTER
- ALTN selector: AUTO
- All lights: OFF
