**Preflight — Overhead Panel (5th Column)**

Temperature control panel:
- TRIM AIR: ON (forward)
- Air temperature knobs: 12:00
- Blue lights: ON × 2; no amber lights unless APU BLEED ON

Bleed / pack panel:
- L/R recirc fans: AUTO
- Isolation valve: OPEN
- Bleed air switches: ON
- If using PCA:
    - Pack switches L/R: OFF
- If using APU bleed:
    - Pack switches L/R: AUTO or HIGH
    - APU BLEED: ON
    - No amber lights except DUAL BLEED

Cabin pressurization:
- FLT ALT and LAND ALT: ENTER
- ALTN selector: AUTO
- All lights: OFF
