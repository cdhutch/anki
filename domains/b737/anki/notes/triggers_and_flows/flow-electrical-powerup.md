**Electrical Power Up Procedure**

- Electrical Panel: 2 black switches guard down (BAT and STANDBY POWER)
- ALT Flaps: red guard down
- Wipers: PARK x 2
- Electric hydraulic pumps OFF × 2
- Gear handle DOWN: 3 green, no red lights
- GND PWR ON
- No electrical amber lights except DRIVE
