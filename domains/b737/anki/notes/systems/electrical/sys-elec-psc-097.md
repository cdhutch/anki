**B737 ELECTRICAL — ELECTRICAL — GENERAL (CROSS-SUBSYSTEM)**

What does the Static Inverter do?

# back_md

Converts 24V DC from the battery to 115V AC to supply the AC standby bus during loss of normal electrical power.
