# front_md

**ENGINES — TAI INDICATION**

What do the **TAI indications** represent?

# back_md

TAI indicates the status of **Thermal Anti-Ice**.

- **Green TAI**
  - engine thermal anti-ice is **operating**

- **Amber TAI**
  - **anti-ice valve disagreement or fault**

- **No TAI indication**
  - thermal anti-ice is **off**
