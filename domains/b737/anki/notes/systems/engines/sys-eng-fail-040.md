# front_md

**ENGINES — LOW OIL PRESSURE**

Why is low oil pressure a serious engine condition?

# back_md

Low oil pressure indicates insufficient lubrication for engine components.

This can result in:

- Increased friction
- Overheating
- Possible engine damage
