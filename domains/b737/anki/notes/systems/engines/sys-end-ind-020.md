# front_md

**ENGINES — EGT**

What does EGT indicate?

# back_md

**EGT (Exhaust Gas Temperature)** indicates the temperature of the exhaust gases leaving the turbine section.

EGT is used to monitor:

- Egine performance  
- Egine limits  
- Egine start conditions
