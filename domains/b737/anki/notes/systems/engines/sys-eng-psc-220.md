# front_md

**ENGINES — STARTER VALVE**

What is the function of the starter valve?

# back_md

The starter valve controls pneumatic air flow to the air turbine starter.

When opened it:

- Allows pneumatic air to drive the starter
- Rotates the engine N2 spool
- Initiates the engine start sequence
