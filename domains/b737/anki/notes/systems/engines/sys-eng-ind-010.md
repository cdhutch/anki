# front_md

**ENGINES — N1**

What does N1 on the primary engine display indicate?

# back_md

**N1** indicates the rotational speed of the **engine fan**.

It is displayed as a **percentage of maximum RPM** and is the **primary thrust reference** used during most flight phases.
