# front_md

**ENGINES — START SYSTEM**

How are the engines started?

# back_md

Engines are started using **pneumatic air** supplied to the **starter motor**.

The air source may be:

- APU bleed air
- External air
- Cross-bleed from the operating engine

The starter rotates the engine until sufficient **N2 speed** is reached for fuel introduction and ignition.
