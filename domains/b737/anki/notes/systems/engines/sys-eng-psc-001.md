# front_md

**B737 ENGINES — EEC**

What is the function of the Electronic Engine Control (EEC)?

# back_md

The Electronic Engine Control (EEC) automatically manages engine thrust by controlling fuel flow.

It provides:

- thrust management  
- engine limit protection  
- optimized engine performance

The EEC controls the engine fuel metering unit to maintain commanded thrust settings.
