# front_md

**ENGINES — IGNITION**

What is the purpose of the engine ignition system?

# back_md

The ignition system provides the **spark required to ignite the fuel-air mixture during engine start**.

Ignition is also used during certain operating conditions requiring **continuous ignition**.
