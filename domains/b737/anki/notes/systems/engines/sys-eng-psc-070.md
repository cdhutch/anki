# front_md

**ENGINES — THRUST CONTROL**

How does the EEC control engine thrust?

# back_md

The **Electronic Engine Control (EEC)** regulates thrust by controlling the **fuel metering unit**.

The EEC adjusts fuel flow to maintain the commanded thrust level.
