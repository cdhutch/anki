# front_md

**ENGINES — EEC SWITCH (MAX-8)**

What positions are available on the **MAX-8 EEC switch**?

# back_md

The **MAX-8 EEC switch** has two positions:

- **NORMAL**
  - Full engine control with protections

- **ALTN**
  - Alternate engine control mode with reduced protections
