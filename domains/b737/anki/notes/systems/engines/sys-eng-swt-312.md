# front_md

**ENGINES — EEC SWITCH (-800)**

What positions are available on the **737-800 EEC switch**?

# back_md

The **737-800 EEC switch** has two positions:

- **ON**
  - EEC operates in **Normal Mode**

- **ALTN**
  - EEC operates in **Alternate Mode**
