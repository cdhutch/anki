# front_md

**ENGINES — START SEQUENCE**

What occurs during a normal engine start?

# back_md

A normal engine start occurs in this sequence:

- Pneumatic air drives the starter motor
- The engine begins rotating (N2 increases)
- Ignition is energized
- Fuel is introduced at the start lever
- Combustion accelerates the engine
