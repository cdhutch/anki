# front_md

**ENGINES — ACCESSORY GEARBOX**

What is the function of the engine accessory gearbox?

# back_md

The accessory gearbox transfers mechanical power from the engine core to drive engine accessories.

These include:

- Fuel pumps
- Oil pumps
- Hydraulic pumps
- Electrical generators
