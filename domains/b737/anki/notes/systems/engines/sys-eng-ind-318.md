# front_md

**ENGINES — REVERSER AIR / GRD LIGHT (MAX-8)**

What does the **REVERSER AIR / GRD** light indicate?

# back_md

The **REVERSER AIR / GRD** light indicates that the **air/ground thrust reverser logic is failed.**.
