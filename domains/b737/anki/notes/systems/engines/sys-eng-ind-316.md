# front_md

**ENGINES — ENGINE CONTROL LIGHT**

What does the **ENGINE CONTROL** light indicate?

# back_md

The **ENGINE CONTROL** light indicates a **fault in the Electronic Engine Control (EEC) system**.

When illuminated:

- Engine control system is **not dispatchable** due to faults in system.
