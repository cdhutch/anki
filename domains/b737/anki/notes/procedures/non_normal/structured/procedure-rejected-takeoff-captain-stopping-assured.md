# front_md

**Rejected Takeoff — Captain — Stopping Is Assured**

What actions does the captain take once stopping is assured during an RTO?

# back_md

**Rejected Takeoff — Captain — Stopping Is Assured**

- Start movement of reverse thrust levers to reach the reverse idle detent before taxi speed
- After engines are at reverse idle, move reverse thrust levers full down

**Memory rule:**  
*Idle reverse → Stow reverse*
