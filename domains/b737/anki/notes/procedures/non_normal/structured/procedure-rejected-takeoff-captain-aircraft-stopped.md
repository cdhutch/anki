# front_md

**Rejected Takeoff — Captain — Aircraft Stopped**

What does the captain do once the aircraft is stopped after an RTO?

# back_md

**Rejected Takeoff — Captain — Aircraft Stopped**

- Assess whether evacuation is required

**If evacuation is NOT required:**
- Make PA: "“This is the captain. Remain seated. Remain seated. Remain seated.”"
- Advise cabin of intentions when able

**If evacuation is immediately required:**
- Call for the Evacuation checklist
- Accomplish the checklist
- Review After Rejected Takeoff considerations

**Memory rule:**  
*Stopped = Assess → Stay or Evacuate*
