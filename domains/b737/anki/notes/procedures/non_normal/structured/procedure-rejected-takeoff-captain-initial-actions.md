# back_md

**Rejected Takeoff — Captain — Initial Actions**

- “Reject, my aircraft.”
- Close thrust levers
- Disengage autothrottle
- Apply maximum braking / verify RTO braking
- Deploy speedbrake
- Apply reverse thrust as conditions permit
- Continue braking until stop assured

**Memory flow:**  
*Idle — A/T off — Max brakes — Boards — Reverse*
