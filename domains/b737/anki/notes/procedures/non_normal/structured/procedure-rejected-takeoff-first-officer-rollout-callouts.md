**Rejected Takeoff — First Officer — Rollout Callouts and Communications**

Required callouts:

- “80”
- “60”
- Any omissions

When practical:

- Communicate reject decision to tower
- Notify appropriate crew members

*Call speed • Call omissions • Communicate*
