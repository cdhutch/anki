**Arrival — Landing Flap Selection**

- Normal landing flaps:
  - 15, 30, or 40
- Flaps 15:
  - Not authorized for LAHSO
- Use Flaps 40 when stopping margin matters:
  - Braking action less than good
  - Tailwind
  - Wet/contaminated runway
  - Runway length < 7500 ft
- If available landing length is 6000 ft or less:
  - Standard landing config: Flaps 40, Autobrake 3 or MAX
