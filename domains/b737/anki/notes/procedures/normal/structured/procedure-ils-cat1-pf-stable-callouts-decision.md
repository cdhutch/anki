# front_md

**ILS CAT I — PF — Stable Callouts / Decision Segment**

What are the PF actions from 1000 AGL through minimums on an ILS CAT I?

# back_md

**ILS CAT I — PF — Stable Callouts / Decision Segment**

- At 1000 AGL:
  - Verify stabilized approach
- At 500 AGL:
  - Verify altitude, speed, and sink rate
- At DA(H), choose one:
  - PM: "No contact."
    - Execute go-around
  - PM: "Approach lights."
  - PM: "Runway."
    - "Continuing."
    - Disengage autopilot NLT 50 feet AGL
