# front_md

**Rejected Landing — Go-Around After Touchdown (Before Thrust Reverser Selection) — PM**

What are the PM actions for a go-around initiated after touchdown but before thrust reverser selection?

# back_md

**Rejected Landing — Go-Around After Touchdown (Before TR Selection) — PM**

- Verify SPEEDBRAKES retracted
- Verify autobrakes disarm
- Verify desired thrust is set
- At V_REF, call out: **"Rotate."**
- When safely airborne:
  - Verify positive rate of climb on altimeter
