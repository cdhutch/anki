# front_md

**Rejected Landing — Go-Around After Touchdown (Before Thrust Reverser Selection) — PF**

What are the PF actions for a go-around initiated after touchdown but before thrust reverser selection?

# back_md

**Rejected Landing — Go-Around After Touchdown (Before TR Selection) — PF**

**Warning:** After reverse thrust is initiated, a full stop landing must be made.

- "Go around."
- Simultaneously:
  - Smoothly advance thrust levers to go-around thrust
  - Maintain landing flap configuration
- At V_REF:
  - Smoothly rotate toward 15° pitch attitude
- When safely airborne:
  - Verify positive rate of climb on altimeter
  - Select TO/GA to attain FD go-around mode
- Continue Normal Go-Around
