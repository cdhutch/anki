# front_md

**Rejected Landing — Go-Around Before Touchdown**

What applies when a go-around is initiated before (or as) touchdown occurs?

# back_md

**Rejected Landing — Go-Around Before Touchdown**

- Continue with normal go-around procedures
- The F/D go-around mode continues to provide guidance throughout
- The takeoff configuration warning horn may sound momentarily if flaps have **not** retracted to Flaps 15 and thrust levers are advanced
