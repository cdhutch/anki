# front_md

**Non-ILS — PF — Stable Callouts / Decision Segment**

What are the PF actions from 1000 AGL through minimums on a Non-ILS approach?

# back_md

**Non-ILS — PF — Stable Callouts / Decision Segment**

- At 1000 AGL:
  - Verify stabilized approach
- At 500 AGL:
  - Verify altitude, speed, and sink rate
- At DA or DDA, choose one:
  - PM: "No contact."
    - Execute go-around
  - PM: "Approach lights."
  - PM: "Runway."
    - "Continuing."
    - Disengage autopilot
