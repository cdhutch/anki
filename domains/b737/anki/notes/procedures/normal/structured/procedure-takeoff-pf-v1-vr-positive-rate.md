# back_md

**Takeoff — PF V1 / VR / Positive Rate**

- At V1: continue the takeoff
- At VR: Rotate at 2° to 3° per second to reach the 10° pitch line on the PFD
- After liftoff and positive rate:
  - "Gear up."
- Target speed: V2 + 20 knots
