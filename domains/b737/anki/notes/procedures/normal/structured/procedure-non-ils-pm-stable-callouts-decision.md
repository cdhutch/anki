# front_md

**Non-ILS — PM — Stable Callouts / Decision Segment**

What are the PM actions from 1000 AGL through minimums on a Non-ILS approach?

# back_md

**Non-ILS — PM — Stable Callouts / Decision Segment**

- At 1000 AGL:
  - Auto callout: "1000."
  - Verify altitude
  - "Stable."
- At 500 AGL:
  - Auto callout: "500."
  - "Stable, target, sink ___."
  - or
  - "Stable, ± ___, sink ___."
- At +100:
  - Auto callout: "Plus hundred."
- At DA or DDA:
  - Auto callout: "Minimums."
- If no runway environment:
  - "No contact."
  - Monitor go-around and perform PM duties
- If approach lights only:
  - "Approach lights."
- If runway environment in sight:
  - "Runway."
- If PF continues:
  - Monitor landing / rollout
