# back_md

**Non-ILS Approaches — Core Guidance Summary**

- **VNAV/LNAV guidance is authorized across non-ILS approaches** per chart logic.
- **LNAV** is the primary roll mode for all RNAV-based approaches.
- **VNAV** is the normal pitch mode where available.
- Some conventional approaches may also use **VOR/LOC** roll guidance.
