# front_md

**Landing Flap Selection** — when is **Flaps 40** recommended?

# back_md

Use **Flaps 40** when stopping margin matters:

- Braking action less than **Good**
- **Tailwind**
- **Wet or contaminated** runway
- Runway length **less than 7,500 ft**
