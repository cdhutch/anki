**Takeoff — Gates and Numbers**

What are the key takeoff gates and associated actions?

# back_md

**Takeoff — Gates and Numbers**

- 40% N1: engines stabilized before TO/GA advance continues
- 80 knots: PF pulse check
- 90 knots: verify A/T annunciation changes to THR HLD
- 400 feet RA: set roll mode
- Thrust REDUCTION height: thrust to CLB 1 / 2
- ACCEL HT: VNAV OR (LVL CHG + set speed)
