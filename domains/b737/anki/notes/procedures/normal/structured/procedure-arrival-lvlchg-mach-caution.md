# front_md

**Arrival — LVL CHG Mach Caution**

What is the Mach caution when using LVL CHG in descent?

# back_md

**Arrival — LVL CHG Mach Caution**

- If aircraft is flying a Mach number when LVL CHG is selected:
  - MCP airspeed displays Mach and may not change to IAS
  - Aircraft will overspeed trying to maintain Mach in descent
  - Autothrottle will not protect from overspeed at idle thrust
- To prevent VMO overspeeds:
  - Manually select IAS with the C/O button on the MCP

**Memory rule:**  
*LVL CHG while in Mach = change to IAS.*
