# back_md

**Discontinued Approach**

1. NAV 1 and NAV 2 — Deselect ILS frequency
2. Altitude — Check/Set
3. V/S — Select/Set
4. Heading — Check/Set
5. HDG or LNAV — Select

Set speed and reconfigure as required.

*Note: May be used in lieu of normal go-around procedures to utilize the autopilot. PF should brief as a go-around consideration.*
