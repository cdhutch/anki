# front_md

**Minimum Drag Trim Setting**

What is the procedure for setting minimum drag trim?

# back_md

**Setup**

1. **FUEL** — Balance within 100 lb
2. **N1s** — Match both sides
3. **ROLL MODE** — HDG SEL

**Execution**

1. Wait **30 seconds**
2. Use **rudder** to move the magnetic heading to the tick mark
