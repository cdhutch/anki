# front_md

**Approach — General — PM — Initial Approach**

What are the PM actions and callouts prior to starting approach and during the initial approach?

# back_md

**Approach — General — PM — Initial Approach**

**Prior to starting approach**
- Ensure waypoints are sequenced properly

**Initial approach**
- Check airspeed
- "Speed verified."
- Select requested flap setting
- Monitor flaps and slats extension
- Set speed if requested
