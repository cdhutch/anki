# front_md

**Visual approach — on final** — what is the target descent rate, and what is the limit?

# back_md

- **Target:** 700–900 fpm
- **Limit:** Descent rates greater than **1,000 fpm** should be avoided
- Stabilize on final approximately **500 feet** above touchdown
