# front_md

During an emergency descent, what are the PF altitude actions?

# back_md

- Select a lower altitude on MCP
- After descent is established, select appropriate altitude
- Descend to **10,000 feet or minimum safe altitude, whichever is higher**
- Continue descent to 10,000 feet when able
