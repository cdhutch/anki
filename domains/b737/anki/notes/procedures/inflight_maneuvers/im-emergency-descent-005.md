# front_md

What are the PM duties during an emergency descent?

# back_md

- Notify ATC and request altimeter setting
- Ensure FASTEN BELTS — ON
- ENGINE START switches — CONT
- Check safe altitude (MEA, MOCA, MORA, MSA, depressurization cloud altitudes)

When cabin altitude descends below 10,000 feet:

- Notify flight attendants
