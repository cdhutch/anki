## Pilot Flying — Continue the Recovery

After the initial stall break, what does the PF do?

# back_md

## PF Continue Recovery

1. Roll in the shortest direction to wings level if needed
2. Advance thrust levers as needed
3. Retract speedbrakes
4. Do not change gear or flap configuration unless a specific published exception applies
