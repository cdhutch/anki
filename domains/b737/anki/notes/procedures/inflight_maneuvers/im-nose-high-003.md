# back_md

## PF Recovery Inputs

1. Apply nose down elevator as needed to obtain a nose down pitch rate
2. Apply appropriate nose down stabilizer trim
3. Reduce thrust
4. Roll (adjust bank angle) as needed to obtain a nose down pitch rate
