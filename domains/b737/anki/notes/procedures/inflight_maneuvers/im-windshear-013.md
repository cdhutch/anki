# front_md

## Predictive Windshear on Takeoff

What actions apply if a predictive windshear caution or warning is received at or above V1?

# back_md

## At or Above V1

### Caution
- Maneuver as required to avoid windshear
- Consider using maximum thrust

### Warning
- Perform the Windshear Escape Maneuver

## Memory Items

Maneuver as required; consider max thrust; warning = escape maneuver
