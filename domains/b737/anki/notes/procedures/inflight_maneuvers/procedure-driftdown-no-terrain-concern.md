# front_md

**At Driftdown Altitude — Terrain Clearance is Not a Concern**

What altitude, thrust and speed are used when terrain clearance is not a concern?

# back_md

**At Driftdown Altitude — No Terrain Concern**

- Thrust: Enough to maintain level cruise
- Altitude: One Engine LRC
- Speed: One Engine LRC
