# front_md

**Emergency Descent — PM Initial Actions**

What are the PM initial actions during an emergency descent?

# back_md

**Emergency Descent**

- ATC: Notify and request altimeter setting
- PA
- **Fasten Belts** — ON
- **Engine Start** switches — CONT
- Verify safe altitude (MEA, MOCA, MORA, MSA)
