# front_md

During an emergency descent, what are the PF airspeed and drag actions?

# back_md

- Select LVL CHG
- Close thrust levers (both)

Then:

- Reduce thrust to minimum or as needed for anti-ice
- Speedbrakes — FLIGHT DETENT
- Set MMO/VMO

If structural integrity is in doubt:

- Limit speed as much as possible
- Avoid high maneuvering loads
