# front_md

**B737 MAX-8 LIMITS — ENGINE ANTI-ICE**

What caution applies to **Engine Anti-Ice operation in flight**?

# back_md

**Do NOT operate Engine Anti-Ice in flight** when **actual or anticipated icing conditions do not exist.**

**Reason**

Operating EAI outside icing conditions may result in:
- **severe engine inlet damage**
- **engine failure**
