# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — ENGINE ANTI-ICE**

When must **Engine Anti-Ice (EAI)** be used during **descent**?

# back_md

EAI **must be ON** when:

- icing conditions **exist**, OR  
- icing conditions are **anticipated**

**Note**

This requirement applies **even when when SAT < 40 °C**
