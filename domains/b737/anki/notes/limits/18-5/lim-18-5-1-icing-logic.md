# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — ICING CONDITIONS**

What constitutes **icing conditions**?

# back_md

Icing conditions exist when **both** of the following are true:

### 1️⃣ Temperature

- OAT (ground/takeoff) ≤ 10 °C
OR
- TAT (in flight) ≤ 10 °C

### 2️⃣ At least ONE of the following

- **Visible moisture is present**

Examples include:
- clouds
- fog (visibility ≤ 1 SM)
- rain
- snow
- sleet
- ice crystals

**OR**

- The airplane is operating on a **contaminated surface**, such as:
- snow
- ice
- standing water
- slush
