# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — ENGINE ANTI-ICE**

When must **Engine Anti-Ice (EAI)** be used during **ground, climb, and cruise** operations?

# back_md

### Ground, Climb and Cruise

EAI **must be ON** when:

- icing conditions **exist**, OR  
- icing conditions are **anticipated**

**Exception**

During **climb and cruise**, EAI may be **OFF** when SAT < -40 °C
