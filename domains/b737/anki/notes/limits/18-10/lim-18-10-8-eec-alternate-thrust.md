# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — EEC ALTERNATE MODE TAKEOFF**

Both EECs are in alternate mode. What thrust rating is required for the **-800** and the **-MAX 8**?

# back_md

**-800 (3AA–3PX)**
26K max takeoff thrust — or 27K max if planned

**-MAX 8 (3RA–3VM)**
TO takeoff thrust
