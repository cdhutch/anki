# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — THRUST**

What is **prohibited when antiskid is inoperative for takeoff**?

# back_md

When **antiskid is inoperative**:

- **Assumed temperature (reduced thrust) takeoff is prohibited**  
- **Full rated takeoff thrust must be used**
