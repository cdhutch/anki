# front_md

**B737 ENGINES — EXTENDED ENGINE MOTORING**

What is **extended engine motoring** used for?

# back_md

Extended engine motoring is used to:

- **Dry the engine core** after a wet start or fuel accumulation  
- **Vent residual fuel** from the combustion section  
- **Cool internal components**

The engine is **motored without fuel or ignition** using the starter.
