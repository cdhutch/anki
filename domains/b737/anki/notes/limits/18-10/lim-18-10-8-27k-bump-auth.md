# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — 27K BUMP THRUST**

Outside KSNA, what authorizes 27K Bump and what selection restrictions apply?

# back_md

**Authorization:**
- Dispatch authorized
- Specified on the load-agent generated TPS
- Pre-authorized routes only, and only when needed to carry payload

**Selection restriction:**
- May **NOT** be selected or requested via ACARS if the TPS is based on any other thrust rating
