# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — REVERSE THRUST**

What **non-memory prohibited actions** are associated with reverse thrust?

# back_md

Prohibited actions:

- **Backing the aircraft** using reverse thrust  
- **Attempting a go-around** after thrust reverser deployment
