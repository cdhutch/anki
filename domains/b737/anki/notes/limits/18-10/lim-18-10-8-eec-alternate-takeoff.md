# front_md

**B737 LIMITS — THRUST**

What limitations apply when **both EECs are in ALTERNATE mode during takeoff**?

# back_md

**737-800**

- 26K max takeoff thrust  
- 27K max if planned

**737 MAX-8**

- Full rated takeoff thrust only

**Additional limitations**

- Reduced or assumed temperature thrust prohibited  
- Do **NOT** use FMC takeoff N1  
- Do **NOT** use FMC QRH V-speeds  
- **Autothrottle must NOT be used for takeoff**
