  Topic: Motion Verbs — до vs в/у vs на for Destination
  Text: Destination is a person → {{c1::до + genitive}}. Destination is an enclosed or bounded
    space being entered → {{c2::в/у + accusative}}. Destination is a surface, open area, or
    route → {{c3::на + accusative}}. For a general place or institution (not a person, not
    emphasized as an enclosed space or a specific route), the first two options above are
    often {{c4::interchangeable}}.
  Extra: 'до is required for a person and is never replaced by в/у or на (зайти до друга,
    до бабусі). в/у marks entry into an enclosed or bounded space (зайти в кімнату, заїхати
    в ліс). на marks a surface, open area, or route (заїхати на бічну вулицю, піти на роботу)
    -- compare до роботи ("until work," not "to work"), which is a different sense of до entirely.
    For a general place or institution, до and в/у genuinely overlap: піти до школи = піти
    в школу. This governs the destination of Ukrainian motion verbs generally (йти/піти, ходити,
    їхати/поїхати, заходити/зайти, заїжджати/заїхати) — it is not specific to за- or any one
    prefix; the prefix changes aspect/manner of motion, not which preposition the destination
    takes. СУМ (via goroh.pp.ua) splits заїжджати into two core senses along exactly the до/в
    split: sense 1 "вʼїжджати всередину, в межі чого-небудь" (в/у) vs sense 2 "заїжджати до
    когось ненадовго" (до); заїжджати also takes на for routes/surfaces (заїхати на бічну
    вулицю).'
  SourceDocument: яблуко

  Source_URL: https://r2u.org.ua/guides/synyavsky/prosti_pryimennyky
  Source_Note: Verified 2026-07-22 against Синявського normative grammar (до = general directional
    prep, в/у + Acc = motion into an interior; піти до школи = піти в школу given as parallel
    constructions), the Dobra Forma UA-as-foreign-language textbook (ch. 7.1/8.2, до obligatory
    for people; на роботу vs до роботи distinction from Dobra Forma's genitive-prepositions
    chapter), and goroh.pp.ua Тлумачення pages for заходити/заїжджати (СУМ sense divisions,
    including заїжджати's на-governed senses for routes/surfaces). Caveat -- for places (not
    people), до and в/у genuinely overlap in real usage (e.g. "зайшла до кімнати" is attested
    even though a room is a canonical enclosed space) -- treat as a strong tendency with a
    categorical case for people, not an absolute rule for every non-person destination.
